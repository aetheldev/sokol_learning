# A07 Game Feel And Juice

Goal: understand that polished feel comes from layered feedback, not one effect.

## Good Juice Stack

For one action, combine:
1. anticipation or snap
2. sound
3. particle burst
4. color flash
5. tiny camera response
6. maybe glow or impact ring

## Examples

Push box:
- small thud sound
- 3 dust particles
- tiny shake
- short white edge flash

Laser hits mirror:
- spark burst
- bright impact point
- short audio click

Card played:
- slide motion
- confirm sound
- highlight pulse on discard pile

## Practice

- pick one action in Sokoban
- pick one action in co-op puzzle
- pick one action in card game
- write the 5-layer feedback stack for each
